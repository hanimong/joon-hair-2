About Us

More than a salon.
An experience.

Joon Hair was founded with a single purpose: to create a space where creativity meets precision. Nestled in Brisbane's vibrant inner-city, our studio is a sanctuary of calm where every visit is a personalised journey.

Our team of expert colourists and stylists bring international training and a genuine passion for craft to every appointment — whether you're after a sun-kissed balayage, a precise cut, or a complete transformation.

Every guest leaves not just looking beautiful, but feeling it too.
